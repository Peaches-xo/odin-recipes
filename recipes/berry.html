Berry Overnight Oats

Description

Prep these fruity, creamy oats the night before and in the morning you've got a quick, fresh breakfast.

Per Serving: 378 calories; protein 17g; carbohydrates 49.5g; fat 14g; cholesterol 25mg; sodium 119.6mg.

Ingredients

Makes one serving

  • 1/2 cup quick oats
  • 1/2 cup non-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup non-fat plain Greek yoghurt
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds (optional)
  • 1 cup fresh mixed berries & fruit

Steps

  1. Add oats to a container of your choice & pour in milk.
  2. Layer Greek yoghurt, chia seeds, mixed fruit & berries.
  3. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning!
