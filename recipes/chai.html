Chai Pomegranate
Overnight Oats

Description

Overnight oats with chai tea latte, vanilla yogurt, honey, and pomegranate seeds make a perfect on-the-go breakfast.

Ingredients

Makes one serving

  • 1/2 cup quick oats
  • 1 chai tea bag
  • 1/4 cup low-fat vanilla Greek yoghurt
  • 1/2 cup low-fat milk
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds

Steps

  1. Add oats to a container of your choice & pour in chai tea latte.

  2. Layer on yoghurt and top with honey and pomegranate seeds.

  3. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning!
